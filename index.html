WHO WE ARE

Ukanafun/Oruk Anam Skills Acquisition and Empowerment Initiative is a not-for-profit organisation founded by the people of Ukanafun/Oruk Anam Federal Constituency in conjunction with Hon. Unyime Idem, Member representing Ukanafun/Oruk Anam Federal Constituency in Nigeria’s House of Representatives to assist its people in skills acquisition, training and empowerment and attracting development through self-help and community efforts
