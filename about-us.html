ABOUT US

 The need to set up this NGO arose following the three year crises that engulfed Ukanafun/Oruk Anam LGAs and its environs leading to the death of more than 50 persons, displacement of more than 500 persons, kidnapping of more than 150 persons and destruction of properties worth hundreds of millions of naira.

The aim of establishing UO-ASAEI is to help people especially those in rural areas overcome barriers to financial security and success. The key element in the vision remains to raise the next generation of entrepreneurs, leaders, patriotic and selfless leaders who would help rebuild their fatherland in all facets and `make life meaningful for themselves and their dependants.

Since its formal launch in October 2015, UO-ASAEI has made great strides, promoted and encouraged the development of initiatives that explains and entrench African brotherhood as entrenched in African Socialism (Ujaama) enunciated by the late Tanzanian President, Julius Nyerere. With the support and partnership of many organizations and a lot of hard work, the organization has gone from strength to strength, becoming a company limited by guarantee, achieving charity.

The idea is to create family, sense of belonging through social and economic programmes where there will be collective responsibility; fostering of cohesion, love and service to one another.

 UO-ASAEI enforces self-reliance where everyone is taught to work for the good of all; do things for themselves and learn to be satisfied with what they have at any given time.
